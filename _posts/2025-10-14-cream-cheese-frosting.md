Cream cheese frosting

8oz block of cream cheese.
1/4cup + 2 tbl olive oil.
Mix 'till fluffy

Then add..
2 1/4 cups powdered sugar.
A "little" cream? (Used 1 tbl)
1/2 tsp vanilla.

NOTE for next time, if there is one. Bump up the vanilla to 1 tsp. Drop the cream.
