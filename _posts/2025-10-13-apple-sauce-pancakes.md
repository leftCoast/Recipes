### Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups flour
* 1+ tbl baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tbl sugar
* 1 cup milk
* 1/4 cup applesauce
* 1 egg
* 2 tbl + 1 tsp olive oil
* REAL maple syrup

### Instructions

1.  Modify the fluffy pancake recipe to this..
2.  Stack finished pancakes with a heaping spoon or two of applesauce between them.
3.  Pour REAL maple syrup over the stack.
4.  Serve.
